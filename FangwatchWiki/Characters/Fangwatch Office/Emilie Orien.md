# Personality
___
Emilie, despite her clan bane, is surprisingly composed. She can be blunt and often prefers to keep to herself, but when needed, she is kind and looks out for others. However, when her bane begins to manifest in full force, she becomes very anxious and paranoid, and constantly stares off into space.

# Biography
___
## Mortal Life

Born and raised in Utah, Emilie never lived outside of the state and worked as professor of Criminal Psychology at BYU. Eventually, she met [[Henry Orien]] and the two were later married, having a family together. One day, Emilie was involved in a bank robbery and developed paranoid schizophrenia as a result. This led to Emilie's family growing closer as they all bonded in their shared role of caretaker. And then Emilie died.

## Embrace

Not much is known about Emilie's embrace, other than she left on the streets shortly afterwards.

## Joining Fangwatch

In Emilie's darkest moment, [[Mina Harker]] came and offered a hand of salvation, and a contract. On this contract was [[The Westenra Clause]], and Emilie signed her life away, seeing no point in living if her family thinks her dead. She still works as an officer to this day, having embraced a [[Willow Sage]] as a Childer at some point in her career.

## Coterie's Arrival

After the arrival of the Coterie at [[The Fangwatch]], she was eventually able to reconnect with [[Henry Orien]].
